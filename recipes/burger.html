Burger

Description

This is a delicous looking bacon cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Burger Patty

Steps

  1. Get your skillet ready to cook patty
  2. Cook patty until your liking
  3. Cook bacon while your patty is cooking
  4. Put cheese on patty so it melts
  5. Put all ingredients together and enjoy your burger!
