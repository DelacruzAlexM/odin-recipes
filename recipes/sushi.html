Sushi

Description

This is a classic sushi roll known as the California Roll. It is a roll that is usually rolled inside out

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cucumber
  • Crab Meat
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Make the sushi rice
  2. Spread the rice
  3. Place a row ofthe Crab Meat, Avocado, and Cucumber on the rice
  4. Roll the sushi up tightly
  5. Cut them in to equal portions and enjoy!
